Weak retrieval-state support with confident error
&
\emph{What is the population in 2010 of they city popular with tourists?}
&
Gold: \emph{8.005 million}.
KG: \emph{2,217}~(\textbf{\texttimes}).
Vanilla: \emph{8.005 million}~(\checkmark).
&
KG follows the chain to the wrong city and then repeats that answer across
all samples
($\mathrm{DSE}=0$, SD-UQ$\approx 0$).
Here the retrieval-state signal is useful:
GPS is high ($0.94$) because the only linkable answer string is reachable
solely through a distant four-hop path, and SEU remains relatively high
($0.67$), which is enough to warn that the graph never became a settled
reasoning substrate.
\\
\midrule
Wrong but locally coherent traversal
&
\emph{Who is the creator of Mickey Mouse's spouse?}
&
Gold: \emph{Walt Disney}.
KG: \emph{Ub Iwerks}~(\textbf{\texttimes}).
Vanilla: \emph{Walt Disney}~(\checkmark).
&
This is the MuSiQue analogue of a wrong but graph-coherent answer.
Output disagreement again collapses to zero, but GPS is also low
($0.0$) because the retrieved path is internally consistent with the wrong
entity choice.
SEU stays comparatively high ($0.75$), which is useful rather than
pathological here:
it correctly warns that the retrieved evidence does not support the generated
answer particularly well, even though output disagreement has already
collapsed.
\\
\midrule
Correct answer despite GPS abstention
&
\emph{What was the percentage that the Dow Jones fell when the US had the highest unemployment rate?}
&
Gold: \emph{54.7\%}.
KG: \emph{54.7\%}~(\checkmark).
Vanilla: \emph{89\%}~(\textbf{\texttimes}).
&
This case cuts the other way.
KG-RAG resolves the chain correctly even though GPS still abstains at $0.5$,
and SEU is still fairly high ($0.72$).
The value of MuSiQue as a stress set is precisely that the graph signal can be
helpful, unhelpful, or merely incomplete depending on where the chain fails. 
\\
